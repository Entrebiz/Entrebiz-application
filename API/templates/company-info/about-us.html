Our Story

Entrebiz is a digital platform with a perfect customer experience at its heart. Our vision is to walk with the customer into the dawn of a new way of digitalization you have never experienced before.

No matter where you are in this world, you can open private/business accounts and transfer funds in real time.

Entrebiz is an innovative idea from a group of experts who has spent long careers in the FinTech industry and has understood the difficulties of a customer to perform smooth transactions. Our team also includes specialists and accomplished professionals from IT, Customer Support, Compliance and many more.

The idea behind Entrebiz germinated a few years ago where we spent adequate time to research, experience and evaluate various transaction systems and the growing need of customers, especially meeting expectations. Entrebiz places our customers at the center of all that we do. We are what we repeatedly do - so we constantly anticipate our customer’s needs and focus on making their lives better. Our ongoing efforts pivot around offering better products, superior customer experience and greater convenience.

What makes Entrebiz different is its customer-centric approach. Driven by People, Product and Process, the brand believes in the motto “Always Value You” by delivering prompt customized services as per your financial needs.

We believe our core values as integrity, commitment, trust and care.

Could you ever imagine your money reaching the destination much before your anticipated time irrespective of the country or continent? Entrebiz makes it possible. Entrebiz takes your transactions beyond borders.

Our journey never ends here. It’s a relentless process to make sure our customers are happy with their transactions. We strive to incorporate emerging technologies to give our customers an everlasting feeling – a lifetime of new transaction experience!
