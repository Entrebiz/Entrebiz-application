OUR SERVICES

Real-time account opening

Opening an Account in Entrebiz is a simple process for Business and Personal customers. You can open account from any part of the world using a valid identification document, a proof of address, email id and a working mobile phone number.

Personal & Business accounts

In Entrebiz, you can open a personal and/or Business accounts based on your requirements.

Personal Accounts

  • Customer can open Personal account in Entrebiz with valid ID proof and address proof along with other details.
  • Once you open a personal account your account will be verified instantly, after that you are entering in to a world of transaction which provide you unlimited opportunities.
  • You can review payment, send payment and hold funds in Entrebiz in any currencies that you wish.
  • You are facilitated with currency conversion, account to account transfer and international wire transfer instantly and globally.
  • You will have the provision to view the statement and download it according to your need with the Web or Mobile application.

Business account

  • Customer can open Business account in Entrebiz with Certificate of Incorporation, valid ID proof of Directors and address proof along with other details.
  • Convert currencies between your accounts for ultra-competitive exchange rates.
  • You can review payment, send payment and hold funds in Entrebiz in any currencies that you wish.
  • Transact with instant fund crediting, worldwide
  • Live tracking of incoming and outgoing transfers.
  • Transfer money to other Entrebiz accounts instantly.
  • Assign responsible multiple users to manage and access your business accounts
